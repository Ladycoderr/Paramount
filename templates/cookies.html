Cookie Policy

What are Cookies

Cookies are small texts sent to your browser by the webpage you visit. It helps a website to remember the information related to your visit. The purpose of these cookies is to understand your search and provide you with the required content related to your search when you visit again on the web page. It helps a website create value for its visitors, enabling it to understand the need and making it easier for visitors to search a web page. Cookies may be used on some pages of the site for us to provide you with a more customised web

Types of cookies we use

Session cookies: These are temporary cookies and these are deleted as soon as you close the browser. It helps us to provide you the best navigation experience on our website. You can always choose to decline session cookies but this may impact your navigation experience.

Analytical Cookies: Analytical cookies may consist of more persistent or temporary cookies. It helps us understand how you use our website and for how long. These cookies don't collect personal identification but help us serve you with the best content.

Functional cookies: Functional cookies record your choice while visiting our website. It remembers the things such as your language selection or browser setting to provide you with an easy and fast interface on our website.

Third party cookies: Third party cookies help us provide you with content on your social media platforms. These cookies are used by third parties to allow them to understand your need and present you the content like texts or videos on their platform. You can disable these cookies in the browser’s privacy setting. Example of third party cookies for the purpose of analytics and similar uses are as below:

Google: Google Analytics is used to track site statistics and user demographics, interests, and behavior on websites. We also use Google Search Console to help understand how our website visitors find our website and to improve our search engine optimization. Social Networks: Third-party social networks that provide interactive plug-ins or social networking features on Global Shares websites, may use cookies or other methods to gather information regarding your use of our websites. The use of such information by a third party depends on the privacy policy available on that social network’s website, which we encourage you to carefully review. Such third parties may use these cookies or other tracking methods for their own purposes by relating information about your use of our site with any of your Personal Data that they may have.

As we are committed to your privacy and safety, we use these cookies to optimize the functionality of our website and present you the content in connection with your choice and preference
